Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about membership.

We typically review and approve applications within 24 hours of submission. You will receive an email notification once your application is processed.

If you made an error, please contact us immediately at +91 00000 00000 with your correct Transaction ID and Application ID.

Currently, each person can hold one active membership at a time. When additional plan types (Gym, Yoga) launch, you may be able to hold multiple plan types simultaneously.

You can renew your membership by submitting a new application before or after expiry. We will send a renewal reminder via email before your membership expires.
